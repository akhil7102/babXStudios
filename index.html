About BabxStudios

BabxStudios is a creative software development studio focused on building simple, powerful, and user-friendly tools for creators, educators, and professionals. Our mission is to design applications that make complex tasks effortless while maintaining high performance and modern design.

We specialize in productivity tools, recording software, and innovative digital solutions that empower users worldwide. With BabxRec, our flagship screen recorder, we aim to deliver a smooth and high-quality recording experience for everyone—whether you're a content creator, gamer, teacher, or professional.

At BabxStudios, we believe in innovation, simplicity, and quality. We're committed to creating tools that are modern and efficient, designing with ease-of-use in mind, and delivering reliable, high-performance software.
